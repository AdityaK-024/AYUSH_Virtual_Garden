About Us

"Bringing the wisdom of traditional medicine to your fingertips"

Our Mission

At the Virtual Herbal Garden, our mission is to bridge the gap between tradition and technology by offering an immersive and interactive platform to explore medicinal plants rooted in AYUSH systems. We are proud to be backed by the Ministry of AYUSH and the All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA).

Our Vision

We aim to make the knowledge of medicinal plants accessible to everyone, from students to practitioners to enthusiasts. Through realistic 3D models, rich educational content, and interactive features, our platform not only educates but also sparks curiosity and respect for the natural world.

Key Features

Interactive 3D Models

Explore lifelike 3D models of medicinal plants, allowing you to see them from every angle.

Educational Content

Access detailed information about each plant, including its traditional uses, benefits, and cultivation methods.

Quizzes and Interactive Learning

Engage in quizzes and interactive activities to reinforce your understanding of medicinal plants.
