Brahmi

Scientific Name: Bacopa monnieri

Uses in Medicine

  • Cognitive Health: Known for enhancing memory and mental clarity.
  • Anti-Anxiety: Reduces stress and promotes relaxation.
  • Neurological Support: Helps in managing neurodegenerative conditions.

Health Benefits

  • Improves focus, attention, and learning capabilities.
  • Rich in antioxidants that protect brain cells from damage.
  • Boosts the body's ability to handle stress naturally.

Helps in These Diseases

  • Alzheimer's Disease: Slows progression and supports cognitive functions.
  • Anxiety Disorders: Alleviates symptoms of chronic stress and anxiety.
  • Epilepsy: May help in reducing seizure frequency.
  • Attention Deficit Disorders: Enhances focus and reduces hyperactivity.
