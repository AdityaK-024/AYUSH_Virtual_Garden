Turmeric

Scientific Name: Curcuma longa

Uses in Medicine

  • Anti-Inflammatory: Reduces inflammation in arthritis and other chronic conditions.
  • Antiseptic: Applied to wounds to prevent infections.
  • Digestive Health: Supports healthy digestion and alleviates bloating.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants that combat free radicals.
  • Boosts immunity and helps in fighting infections.
  • Promotes heart health by improving cholesterol levels.

Helps in These Diseases

  • Arthritis: Reduces joint pain and stiffness.
  • Diabetes: Aids in controlling blood sugar levels.
  • Skin Conditions: Treats acne, eczema, and other skin ailments.
  • Liver Disorders: Supports liver detoxification and improves function.
