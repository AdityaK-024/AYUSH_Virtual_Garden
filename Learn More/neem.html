Neem

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

Uses in Medicine

  • Skin Care: Treats acne, eczema, and other skin conditions.
  • Dental Health: Used in toothpaste and mouthwash for gum health.
  • Anti-Parasitic: Effective against intestinal worms and parasites.

Health Benefits

  • Purifies blood and supports detoxification.
  • Boosts the immune system to fight infections.
  • Helps in regulating blood sugar levels.

Helps in These Diseases

  • Skin Disorders: Treats psoriasis, ringworm, and other fungal infections.
  • Diabetes: Assists in managing blood glucose levels.
  • Arthritis: Reduces inflammation and pain in joints.
  • Dental Issues: Prevents gum diseases and promotes oral health.
