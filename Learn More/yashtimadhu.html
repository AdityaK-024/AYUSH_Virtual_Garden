Plant Name: Yashtimadhu

Scientific Name: Glycyrrhiza glabra

Common Names: Licorice, Yashtimadhu, Sweetwood

Origin: Native to Europe and parts of Asia, Yashtimadhu is widely cultivated in temperate regions around the world.

Uses in Medicine: Yashtimadhu is well-known for its soothing properties, particularly in treating respiratory issues. It has been used in traditional medicine to treat cough, sore throat, and digestive problems due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Health Benefits:

  • Relieves respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and sore throat.
  • Supports digestive health by alleviating indigestion and stomach ulcers.
  • Has anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce symptoms of arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.
  • Boosts immunity and has antiviral properties to help fight infections.
  • Promotes skin health by reducing irritation and promoting wound healing.

Helps in These Diseases:

  • Respiratory conditions: Cough, sore throat, bronchitis, and asthma.
  • Digestive disorders: Indigestion, stomach ulcers, and acid reflux.
  • Arthritis: Reduces inflammation and pain associated with joint conditions.
  • Skin conditions: Irritation, eczema, and wound healing.
