Plant Name: Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Scientific Name: Ocimum tenuiflorum

Uses in Medicine: Used in Ayurvedic treatments for respiratory ailments, skin infections, and as an immunity booster.

Health Benefits: Improves digestion, reduces stress, enhances immunity, and fights bacterial and fungal infections.

Helps in These Diseases: Asthma, cold, flu, bronchitis, and cardiovascular conditions.
