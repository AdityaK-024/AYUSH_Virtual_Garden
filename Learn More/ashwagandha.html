Ashwagandha

Scientific Name: Withania somnifera

Uses in Medicine

  • Stress Relief: Known as an adaptogen, it helps manage stress and anxiety.
  • Boosts Energy: Enhances stamina and physical performance.
  • Cognitive Health: Improves memory and concentration.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants, supporting overall health and vitality.
  • Improves sleep quality and combats insomnia.
  • Supports the immune system and reduces inflammation.

Helps in These Diseases

  • Anxiety and Depression: Alleviates symptoms by regulating cortisol levels.
  • Thyroid Disorders: Helps balance thyroid hormones.
  • Arthritis: Reduces joint inflammation and pain.
  • Diabetes: May help control blood sugar levels.
