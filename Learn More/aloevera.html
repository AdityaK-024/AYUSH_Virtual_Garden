Aloe Vera

Scientific Name: Aloe barbadensis miller

Uses in Medicine

  • Skin Treatments: Heals burns, wounds, and treats skin conditions like eczema and psoriasis.
  • Digestive Health: Relieves constipation and supports a healthy gut.
  • Oral Care: Reduces gum inflammation and fights plaque in oral hygiene products.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in vitamins (A, C, E, and B12), antioxidants, and enzymes.
  • Boosts immunity and promotes overall health.
  • Aids hydration and detoxification.

Helps in These Diseases

  • Diabetes: May lower blood sugar levels under supervision.
  • Skin Conditions: Treats acne, cold sores, and minor cuts.
  • Digestive Disorders: Manages IBS and acid reflux.
  • Inflammatory Diseases: Provides relief in arthritis and reduces swelling.
