Medicinal Plants

Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Known for its medicinal properties, Tulsi is used to treat colds, coughs, and respiratory issues.

Neem

Neem is used for skin care and to improve digestion. Its antibacterial properties are highly valued.

Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is used for its stress-relieving and energy-boosting properties.

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is used in skincare and for treating burns and improving digestion.

Brahmi

Brahmi is known for enhancing memory and cognitive abilities, used in Ayurvedic medicines.

Turmeric

Turmeric is an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant commonly used to improve skin and digestion.
